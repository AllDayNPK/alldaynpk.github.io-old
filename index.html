Conversion hints

Phosphorus (P)

Fertilizer bags measure the amount of phosphorus as P2O5

1 pound of P2O5 = 0.44 pound of P

2.3 pound of P2O5 = 1 pound of P

Potassium (K)

Fertilizer bags measure the amount of potassium as K2O

1 pound of K2O = 0.83 pound of K

1.2 pound of K2O = 1 pound of K
